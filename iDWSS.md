## Major functions of iDWSS

1. Compliance Monitoring
    - RISC Form
    - Site Safety Inspection 
    - Cleansing Inspection
2. Safety
    - Near miss Report
    - Incident Report
    - Safety Convictions
    - Accident Rate
3. Planning and Productivity
    - Number of Contractor's staff, labour and plant
    - Wage Rate and Number of Workers per trade
4. Quality of Works
    - Success/fail Rate of Submissions
    - Revisions of RISC Form
    - Inspection Fail Rate
5. Process Efficiency
    - Overdue RISC Form
    - Delayed Approval of RISC Form
    - Overdue Site Diary
    - Late Rectification of Non-compliance
